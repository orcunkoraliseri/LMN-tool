Layer 1: Energy Selection Documentation

Overview

The Energy Selection page (layer1_energy_selection.html) allows users to configure consumption and generation parameters.

Parameters

Energy Consumption

  • Electric

Energy Generation

  • PV (Roof/Facade)
  • PV-T (Roof/Facade)
  • STC (Roof/Facade)
  • Biomass, Wind, Geothermal

Data
